About Us

Welcome to Heaven on Earth Restaurant and Bar, where culinary excellence meets warm hospitality. Nestled in the heart of Bo City (2 Bojon Street) opposite the Egyptian Clinic with about a thirty seconds work from the Central Police Station to the central business area of the city, we are proud to be a cherished dining destination for both locals and visitors alike.

Our Story

Heaven on Earth was founded by Madam Florence Mansaray in 2015. With a passion for fine dining and a commitment to creating memorable culinary experiences, CEO Francess Mansaray embarked on a culinary journey that would soon become a cornerstone of Bo gastronomic scene.

Our Philosophy

At Heaven on Earth, our philosophy is simple yet profound: to serve exceptional food in an inviting and elegant ambiance. We believe in the power of food to bring people together, and each dish that leaves our kitchen is a testament to our dedication to quality, creativity, and flavor.

The Culinary Team

Our talented team of chefs, led by Executive Chef Francis Godsent Kainessie, is the driving force behind our culinary success. With a diverse range of culinary backgrounds and a shared passion for innovation, they collaborate to craft a menu that marries classic flavors with contemporary twists.

Farm-to-Table Commitment

We take pride in our commitment to sourcing the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients whenever possible. Our relationships with local farmers and producers allow us to create dishes that not only taste exceptional but also support our community and sustainable agriculture.

Events and Private Dining

Looking to host a special event or celebration? We cater for both within our restaurant and other locations.

Join Us
